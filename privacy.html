Color Match Shot

Privacy information

This page explains—in everyday language—how the Color Match Shot mobile game treats information when you play. It is a general summary for players, not a contract. Effective date: May 6, 2026.

  • Focus: offline-friendly puzzle play
  • Store: distributed via the App Store
  • Labels: check the listing when you install or update

What this covers

The Color Match Shot application as offered on Apple’s App Store. Hardware features, system backups, and Apple account services follow Apple’s settings and terms—not this page.

Player data collection

According to the App Store privacy details published for this title at the time of this writing, the product is represented as not collecting data in the categories Apple uses for its privacy questionnaire.

If a future update changes what the store displays—new features, integrations, or regional requirements—the on-store privacy labels for that version are the disclosure you should rely on when you download or update.

On-device storage

Like most games, Color Match Shot may keep progress, preferences, or temporary files locally so levels load quickly and your choices persist between sessions. That information ordinarily remains on your device unless you use platform backup or transfer tools outside the app.

Platform, analytics, and ads

Installation, updates, parental controls, and purchases are handled by Apple. If a build includes analytics or advertising components, their behavior is governed by the SDKs present in that release and by the privacy controls available in iOS and iPadOS.

  • Review App Tracking Transparency and privacy settings on your device for ad-related choices.
  • Screen Time and Ask to Buy help guardians manage access for younger players.

Younger audiences

The game is listed with a family-friendly age rating on the store. Adults should use the device and account tools they already trust to supervise play time and purchases.

Updates to this summary

When gameplay or store disclosures change in a meaningful way, this overview may be revised and the effective date at the top adjusted. Keeping the App Store page open while installing updates is the simplest way to notice label changes.

Reminder: This document does not include mailing addresses, email contacts, or links to unrelated services. For the privacy nutrition facts tied to the binary you install, refer to the App Store product page for Color Match Shot at download time.
